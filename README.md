## ğŸš€ How to Play

1. **Open the Game**  
   Visit the [live demo](https://arkojyoti27dey.github.io/avatar-tic-tac-toe/).

2. **Upload Avatars**
   - Player 1 clicks **"Upload Image"** and selects their picture.
   - Player 2 does the same.

3. **Start Game**  
   Once both preview images appear, click the **"Start Game"** button.

4. **Play!**  
   Take turns clicking on an empty square to place your avatar.

5. **Win**  
   The first player to get three of their avatars in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) wins!

6. **Play Again**  
   Click **"Play Again"** after a win or draw, or **"Restart"** to clear the board anytime.
